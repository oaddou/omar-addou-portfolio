About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Omar, a seasoned supply chain professional based in The Netherlands with over ten years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry mainly in Supply Chain and Manufacturing. I have a strong foundation in GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) and GDP (Good Distribution Practices), ensuring compliance and maintaining exceptional quality in every operation. I specialize in leveraging SAP for supply chain management and have knowledge of MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems) like Körber PAS-X (Werum). My expertise includes managing deviations, implementing CAPAs (Corrective and Preventive Actions), and overseeing change controls using platforms such as TrackWise and Infinity.

Beyond my supply chain expertise, I am deeply passionate about technology, programming AI. I am currently learning Python, HTML, CSS and JavaScript, with the goal of applying these skills to create innovative solutions within the pharma supply chain and other industries. My goal is to combine my industry knowledge with my growing programming abilities to streamline processes, improve efficiency, and contribute to digital transformation.

My professional journey includes roles such as Lead Material Handler at Bristol-Myers Squibb, Manufacturing Specialist at Janssen Biologics, Supply Chain Specialist at Kite Pharma. In each role, I have demonstrated my ability to manage inventories, coordinate effectively with cross-functional teams, created and managed deviations, perform User Acceptance Testing (UAT) for new systems, and drive process improvement initiatives.

I am always eager to explore new challenges and collaborate on projects that require a blend of technical expertise and supply chain knowledge. Feel free to browse through my projects or connect with me to discuss potential opportunities.
